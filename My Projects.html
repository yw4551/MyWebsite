About the project

The project was done on the following website: https://www.saucedemo.com/ . This website is a demo of an E-commerce website. On the website, you can order items, and you can go through the whole process of ordering items from signing in to the system until you reach the thank-you page.
The website contains a login page so we can login to our account, and it also has a products page with a list of all the products that you can order, a product page with the info of the product for etching one of the products, a shopping cart page where you can store your shopping items, and you should be able to add products to the cart from the products page and the product page. The website should also have a checkout page where you can add your information and an overview page that will want you to confirm all of the information that you put in, and the last page should be a thank you page with an option to go back to the home page.
In the menu options, we also have an option to go back to the products page, an option to see more information about the website, a logout option, and an option to reset the system and empty the cart page.
